A product designer

Born in Macau and raised in Taiwan, I majored in visual communication design in college. My graduation project "The Multimedia interactive interface of pregnancy" got the prize of NAGOYA DESIGN DO!. Since that I have believed in that "Human Centered Design" can improve human well-being, user satisfaction, accessibility and sustainability maintain a sustainable business model.

UI/UX Case Study

I started my career as a UI designer in a small digital marketing agency, a few years later, I became a senior UI designer at the largest retail chain bookstores in Taiwan. Now, I'm a product designer, my job is to identify the users' goals and to create flowcharts, wireframes and interactive prototypes for the product or service, then build a positive and consistent brand image and communicate the right information to the users.
